Strategic Financial Consulting

Strategic financial diagnostic

A clear snapshot of your business: margins, liquidity, cost structure, leverage, and bottlenecks. We deliver immediate recommendations and priorities to organize and improve results. Typical duration: 2–3 weeks.

Forecasting & cash flow planning

We build a tailored financial model with cash flow, P&L, and balance forecasts, pressure‑test scenarios, and validate assumptions with your team. Includes capital needs analysis (working capital, CAPEX, debt) and periodic follow‑ups.

Restructuring & profitability improvement

For companies under cash pressure or with deteriorated margins: we optimize fixed/variable costs, identify unprofitable lines, redefine pricing and break‑even, and design a phased plan with a strong focus on liquidity and operating efficiency.

Fundraising readiness

We prepare you to approach banks or investors professionally: business plan and investment case, capital structure and use of funds, plus executive materials (pitch and clear financials) for a confident fundraising process.

CFO-as-a-Service

Monthly, senior‑level financial support without hiring a full‑time CFO: KPI monitoring, decision support, coordination with accountants, banks or partners, and a planning cadence to keep the financial and operating course.

Business valuation

We estimate company value using DCF and comparable multiples and integrate scenario analysis. Useful for partial sales, partner entry, successions, or strategic negotiations, with transparent assumptions and sensitivities.
